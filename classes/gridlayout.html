A Widget that holds Widgets and the amount of rows and columns, and is to be displayed as a grid on the client

example

new GridLayout({width:2,height:3},[
    new Blank(), new Blank(),
    new Blank(), new Blank(),
    new Blank(), new Blank()
])
//JSX
<GridLayout width={2} height={3}>
     <Blank/> <Blank/>
     <Blank/> <Blank/>
     <Blank/> <Blank/>
</GridLayout>
